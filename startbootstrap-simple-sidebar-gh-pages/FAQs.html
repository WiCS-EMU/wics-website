FAQs

What is this?

    The Women in Computer Science Club at EMU is comprised of female students at EMU who are interested in the field of Computer Science, and the men who support their goals of increasing female participation in Computer Science. This club was founded by our faculty advisor Dr. Krish. Narayanan, and it is run and managed by our volunteer students

What do we do?

    We aim to do the following:
    • Create a supportive environment for all females studying computer science here at EMU, with the goal of encouraging more women to stay and thrive in the field of technology.
    • Bring female software developers to EMU for Speaker Series events.
    • Engage in community outreach by teaching young people to code - with a particular emphasis on girls and women.

Why join us?

    Once you join us you will be provided with:
    1. Professional development activities such as:
      • Field trips to successful technology companies,
      • Guest lectures from successful women in technology,
      • Resume building workshops, Interview workshops, and more!
    2. Networking:
      • Make connections with other students like you.
      • Remember that networking is one of the best steps a successful student can take - not to mention that learning Computer Science is more fun when you're surrounded by friends!
    3. Fun!!
      • Yes, we do organize activities like Holiday parties/End-of-School year Potlucks, and movie nights.
      • Please contact us if you would like to give your own suggestion or want to see more activities organized.
    4. LBC credits.
      • By serving as a board member/officer for one acadamic year you can satisfy the following : (Group 2): Community Service, Citizenship and Leadership: -(Option 2): Serve on executive board of a recognized student organization (for one year). for more onformation visit the LBC Undergraduate Catalog.

What should you do?

    Sign up to join our email list! We'll send you an update about each of our upcoming events/meetings/activities, and you can be as involved as you want to be. Although we strongly encourage everyone to come and actively participate on a regular basis, you can drop by any time we're meeting, even if all you want to do is grab a snack and meet fellow Computer Science students.

Want to be more involved?

    This is a volunteer-based organization, and we'd love for you to join us at any time to help with any of our upcoming activities. Alternatively, if you have an activity in mind, we'd love to help you host it! If you are interested in volunteering or want to inquire about possible community/outreach volunteer opportunities, please contact us.
